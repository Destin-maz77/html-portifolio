The Best Movies According to Destin Mazoma

My top 4 movies / series of all-time

Prison Break

One of the best series I have ever watched. The story, the intelligence, and the escape plans make it very addictive.

Ip Man

A powerful martial arts movie based on the life of Bruce Lee’s master. The fighting scenes and discipline are incredible.

Fast & Furious 10

An action-packed movie full of speed, cars, and strong family themes. Very entertaining from start to finish.

Hobbs & Shaw

A great action movie starring The Rock. Funny, powerful, and full of intense action scenes.
